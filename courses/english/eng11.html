🗣️ Word Stress Mastery Guide

JAMB 2026 • stress rules • suffix patterns • common traps • exceptions • easy learning

📋 Quick Overview

JAMB tests word stress — the syllable that is said more strongly, more clearly, and often a little louder or longer than the others.

  • Stressed syllable = louder, longer, clearer
  • Monosyllabic words = only one syllable, so that syllable takes the stress
  • Polysyllabic words = words with 2 or more syllables
  • JAMB loves suffix rules and exceptions

Simple meanings you must know

Penultimate = second syllable from the end.

Antepenultimate = third syllable from the end.

🧠 Easy trick

If a word has many syllables, count backward from the end. That is how many JAMB stress rules work.

1️⃣ How to Hear Stress Easily

What stress sounds like
The stressed syllable sounds stronger.
Example: ecoNOMic → the stress is on NOM.
How to practise
Say the word slowly.
Clap once on the stressed syllable.
Example: e-co-NOM-ic → clap on NOM.

Best practice method

  • Break the word into syllables
  • Say it aloud
  • Make one syllable stronger
  • Repeat until it sounds natural

2️⃣ Monosyllabic Words

These words have only one syllable, so the stress is naturally on that only syllable.

cat book run pen dog

Important: Monosyllabic words do not give stress trouble in JAMB. The real challenge is with long words.

3️⃣ Group 1 — Stress Normally on the Syllable Before the Suffix

This means the stress usually falls on the syllable immediately before endings like -ic, -tion / -sion, and -ian / -ious.

-ic

Normal rule: stress before -ic
ecoNOMic draMAtic scienTIFic hisTORic symboLIC
JAMB-tested exceptions
These do not follow the normal rule exactly, so memorise them.
ARabic LUNatic poLITic RUbic caTHOLic herOIc poETIC cliNIcal critiCAL voCALic

-tion / -sion

Normal rule: stress before -tion / -sion
examinaTION educaTION informaTION populaTION navigaTION
JAMB-tested exceptions / trap forms
teleVISion quesTIONnaire senSAtional moTIvation diRECtive proMOTional revoLUtion transLAtion conVERsion proGRESsion

-ian / -ious

Normal rule: stress before -ian / -ious
musiCIAN poliTIcian vaRIous seRIous amBItious
JAMB-tested exceptions
barBARian guarDIan paTRIcian pleBEian deLIcious reLIgious nuTRItious amBIguous deLIrious caPRIcious

4️⃣ Group 2 — Stress Normally on the Third Syllable from the End

This means you count backward from the end and place stress on the antepenultimate syllable.

-ate

Normal rule: stress on the third syllable from the end
comMUnicate inVEStigate deMONstrate arTIculate cerTIFicate
JAMB-tested exceptions
transLATE diCTATE roTATE transPORT proCREATE reCREATE deVIATE reNEgate proPAGATE conGREgate

-ity

Normal rule: stress before -ity
responsiBILity uniVERsity maJORity possiBILity perSONality
JAMB-tested exceptions
soCIety vaRIety anxIety noBILity moBILity duRABility viTAlity hospiTAlity caPAcity puRIty

-ical

Normal rule: stress on the third syllable from the end
poLITical critiCAL hisTORical ecoNOMical meDIcal
JAMB-tested exceptions / trap words
voCALical cliNIcal radiCAL mediCAL logiCAL typiCAL classiCAL magIcal fanTAstical muSIcal

5️⃣ Group 3 — Stress on the Last Syllable

Some suffixes attract stress to themselves, especially -ee and -eer.

-ee

Normal rule: stress on -ee
employEE referEE refugEE guarantEE addresSEE
JAMB-tested exceptions / trap words
COFfee TOWee comMITtee TRUStee NOMinee TRAINee APPointee ABSENtee PAYee LEGatee

-eer

Normal rule: stress on -eer
enginEER marketEER auctionEER mountainEER pioneEER
JAMB-tested exceptions / trap words
PRIVateer SONeers CAreer VEneer MUteer BUccaneer CHeer PIoneer DOMineer MOneer

8️⃣ Mini Self-Quiz

1. Where is the stress in economic?
A. E
B. CO
C. NOM
D. IC
Answer: C — ecoNOMic.
2. Which word is an exception to the normal -ic stress rule?
A. dramatic
B. historic
C. Arabic
D. symbolic
Answer: C — ARabic.
3. Which word has stress on the last syllable?
A. coffee
B. employee
C. society
D. lunatic
Answer: B — employEE.
4. Which word is stressed before -tion?
A. examination
B. television
C. committee
D. society
Answer: A — examinaTION pattern means stress before the suffix.
5. Which word is one of the famous JAMB exceptions?
A. communicate
B. nationality
C. coffee
D. engineer
Answer: C — COFfee is a famous trap word.

9️⃣ Quick JAMB Revision Tips

Best way to score high

  • Learn the normal rule first
  • Then learn the common exceptions
  • Clap the stressed syllable aloud
  • Revise famous trap words daily
  • Practise with past JAMB questions from 2018–2025

Winning formula: Rule first, exception second. Never apply a rule blindly without checking whether the word is a known trap.
