🧠 Main goal

Master antonyms and boost your Use of English score.

1️⃣ How to Score 9–10/10 in Antonyms

  • Read the full sentence first
  • Context changes meaning
  • Eliminate synonyms or neutral options first
  • Pick the strongest opposite, not just a different word
  • Many words repeat year after year: benevolent, obsolete, lucid, hostile, candid, timid...

Study method: learn 20 words per day, make your own sentences, then test yourself weekly.

7️⃣ Self-Quiz

1. Benevolent = kind. What is the antonym?
A. Generous
B. Malevolent
C. Friendly
D. Honest
Answer: B — Malevolent.
2. Arduous means difficult. The antonym is
A. Hard
B. Long
C. Easy
D. Rough
Answer: C — Easy.
3. Hostile means unfriendly. The antonym is
A. Harsh
B. Friendly
C. Weak
D. Quiet
Answer: B — Friendly.
4. Scarce means not enough. The antonym is
A. Rare
B. Empty
C. Abundant
D. Small
Answer: C — Abundant.
5. Timid means shy. The antonym is
A. Careful
B. Reserved
C. Quiet
D. Bold
Answer: D — Bold.
