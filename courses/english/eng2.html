🧠 Main goal

Master synonyms and score very high in Use of English.

1️⃣ How to Score 9–10/10 in Synonyms

  • Read the full sentence first
  • Context may change the expected meaning
  • Eliminate opposites and neutral distractors
  • Choose the closest word in meaning
  • Many words repeat year after year: benevolent, lucid, candid, diligent, hostile, timid...

Study method: learn 20 words daily, write simple sentences with them, then test yourself every week.
