Topics

Polynomials

Expressions of variables with non-negative integer exponents, their roots, factors, and algebraic manipulations.

Variation

Relationships where one quantity changes proportionally with another—direct, inverse, and joint proportionality.

Inequalities

Mathematical statements of relative magnitude between expressions, their solutions, and graphical representations on number lines or coordinate planes.

Progressions

Sequences of numbers following predictable patterns—arithmetic (constant difference) and geometric (constant ratio)—and their summation formulas.

Binary Operations

Mathematical rules combining two elements from a set to produce another element within the same set, governed by properties of closure, associativity, commutativity, identity, and inverses.

Matrices & Determinants

Rectangular arrays of numbers and their scalar-valued functions used to solve linear systems, transform spaces, and determine invertibility.
