What is equilibrium?

An object is in equilibrium when the resultant force acting on it is zero.

  • Dynamic equilibrium: object moves with constant velocity

2ÔłŹ‚É£ Triangle and Polygon of Forces

Triangle Law

If three forces acting at a point are in equilibrium, they can be represented in magnitude and direction by the three sides of a triangle taken in order.

Polygon Law

If more than three forces act at a point in equilibrium, they form a closed polygon when drawn head-to-tail.

If the vector diagram closes completely, the forces are in equilibrium.
